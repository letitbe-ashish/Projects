About CityZen

What is CityZen?

CityZen is a smart web platform designed to bridge the communication gap between citizens and local municipal authorities. It empowers users to report, track, and escalate urban issues such as potholes, garbage, water leaks, broken streetlights, and more.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make urban communities cleaner, safer, and more responsive by providing a simple yet effective way for people to raise their voices and take part in civic management. CityZen encourages transparency, accountability, and active citizen participation.

Key Features

  • Easy issue reporting with photo, type, and description
  • Live tracking of issue status and location
  • Voting system to prioritize critical problems
  • Escalation option for unresolved reports
  • Separate admin and user dashboards

Who Built CityZen?

This project is built by a passionate team of students committed to improving civic communication using modern web technologies.
